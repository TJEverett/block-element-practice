OFOSA Adoption Process

Below is the basic proccess of adoption from OFOSA

  1. View available pets

    2. All pets listed on website

  2. Meet the animals and Adoption Counselor

    3. All members of the family (people and pets) must meet the animal to make sure they are a good fit before adopting.

  3. Complete the adoption

    4. If you choose to adopt, you will be able to bring them home that same day. Feel free to ask volunteers or fosters for advice about training or helping the animal adjust to it's new home.

    Medical or Behavior Consults: If the pet you're interested in has any medical conditions, we will arrange pre-adoption consultations with our medical / clinic staff so you know what to expect
